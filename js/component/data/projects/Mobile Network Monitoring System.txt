	</div><div style="text-align: justify;"></p>&emsp;&ensp;In the year of 2015, with the increasing costs and old equipment maintenance budget, CERN decided to switch Mobile Network operator from Sunrise to Swisscom.  This change had a dramatic effect at CERN due to the positive changes in terms of costs and accessibility to the general user, but also, brought some issues to the Telecommunications Team such as: <p><ul id="first"> <li>Troubleshooting new equipment;</li><li>Troubleshoot general network access;</li><li>Create new documentation for the general user;</li><li>Performance analysis within Switzerland and France - <b>&emsp;&ensp;Public APN</b>;</li><li>Performance analysis and troubleshooting - <b>&emsp;&ensp;CERN's private APN</b>;</li><li>SIM card settings tuning;</li><li>Tunnel Leaky Feeder network analysis and decay statistics.</li></p><img src="img/portfolio/data/cern-it-department.gif" style="display: block; margin: 0 auto;"></img></p>&emsp;&ensp;With these goals in mind, CERN acquired a Mobile Monitoring solution of SwissQual&reg; which had to be implemented from the ground up with CERN's envisioned policies: <ul><li><b>Secure access</b> - SFTP, Encryption, etc.;</li><li><b>24/7 Availability</b> and <b>Sustainability</b>;</li><li><b>"Real-time"</b> Remote Probes monitoring/troubleshooting.</li></ul><p></p></div>
